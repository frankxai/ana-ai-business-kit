# Ana AI Business Kit instructions

This is a human-led HR operating kit, not an autonomous recruiting, legal, finance, publishing, or clinical system.

- Keep client material private by default. Never add real client names, transcripts, contact details, candidate or employee data, medical information, employment records, credentials, or payment details to Git.
- Draft only from material Ana or the accountable team member explicitly provides. Do not invent biography, qualifications, testimonials, outcomes, pricing, policy, research claims, or evidence.
- Keep AI backstage: Ana or another named accountable human approves every client-facing output, hiring judgment, commercial term, invoice, publish action, and external send.
- Do not rank, reject, diagnose, profile, or infer candidates or employees from protected traits, personality proxies, emotion, neuroscience, or medical information. Team-development research may inform a voluntary, non-clinical workshop draft; it is never a selection signal or diagnosis.
- Treat persuasion as clear, truthful communication: no fabricated urgency, guarantees, testimonials, or undisclosed manipulation.
- The public repository contains only blank, public-safe material. A separately initialized private company overlay holds Ana's real templates, policies, records, and approved source links; it is not a "private fork" of this public repository.
- Preserve the two-package audience boundary. `ana-operator-kit` is practitioner-only and non-client-sendable; `ana-client-session-kit` is the only client-sendable package after Ana's review.
- Run `npm test` after changes to manifests, package lists, templates, skills, or validation code. Run `npm run package` before a release when machine capacity permits.
