# Adapt this kit for another HR agency

This kit can become a reusable agency operating template without carrying Ana's name, client data, templates, or voice into the new agency.

## Keep the architecture, replace the identity

Copy the public-safe structure, then create a separate private company overlay for the new agency:

```text
public operating kit
        +
private company overlay (policy, real links, approved language)
        +
controlled Drive / ATS / finance systems
        =
human-led agency operating system
```

Replace:

- business name, visual identity, service catalog, currencies, tax/legal advice route, retention policy, and client communications;
- team roles and access with the agency's actual accountability model;
- real template registry only inside the private company overlay;
- legal/commercial terms only after the agency's qualified review.

Keep:

- one private engagement record per client and role;
- the SOP order and explicit exception states;
- source-backed facts, structured job-relevant evidence, and named human decisions;
- copied master templates, visual check, and fresh send gate;
- research and content approval boundaries;
- the fictional practice run before live rollout.

## Minimum viable rollout

1. Name the manager/approver and three operating seats.
2. Create the Drive folder and permission structure.
3. Put master templates and real links only in a private overlay/controlled Drive.
4. Localize one fictional engagement and run it end to end.
5. Pilot one live client with explicit approval gates.
6. Review evidence, errors, and team feedback before scaling.

## Do not copy blindly

This is operational scaffolding, not legal, tax, immigration, employment-law, medical, psychological, or financial advice. Every agency must validate its own jurisdiction, client contracts, data-processing arrangements, approved systems, and regulatory obligations before live use.
