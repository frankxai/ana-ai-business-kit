# Start here: one real offer in one hour

## First 10 minutes — choose the next real thing

Write three bullets in a new note:

- **Offer:** clarity session, reflection circle, or self-knowledge workshop.
- **Human outcome:** what should feel clearer or more usable by the end?
- **Boundary:** what this offer is not (for example, not therapy, diagnosis, legal advice, or an employment decision).

## Next 20 minutes — prepare, do not automate

Use [SESSION-PREP.md](prompts/SESSION-PREP.md) to make a draft agenda and questions. Delete anything that sounds unlike Ana. Keep only questions she would responsibly ask.

## Next 20 minutes — make the client packet

Personalize `../ana-client-session-kit/CLIENT-WELCOME.md` and `SESSION-REQUEST.md`. Review the packet for names, dates, and claims. Send it manually.

## Last 10 minutes — set the follow-up

After the session, use [AFTERCARE-DRAFT.md](prompts/AFTERCARE-DRAFT.md) with only the notes Ana has decided are appropriate to use. The final note must be reviewed and sent by Ana.

If a task is not attached to a scheduled session, stop. The kit earns its value through one reliable client loop, not through producing more documents.
