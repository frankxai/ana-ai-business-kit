# Privacy and scope

## Keep private

Client names, contact details, recordings, raw session notes, employment records, health information, legal material, payment data, and private messages belong in the practitioner’s approved client system—not in this public repository or a shared demo workspace.

## Use AI carefully

Give an AI only the minimum material required for one draft. Generalize and de-identify examples. Treat its output as untrusted until reviewed. Do not enable broad connectors or browser access for a task that only needs a session outline.

## Keep the scope honest

This kit supports reflection, organization, and communication. It does not replace licensed clinical, medical, legal, financial, or HR decision-making support. For urgent safety concerns, use appropriate local emergency or qualified professional support.
