# Circle or workshop workflow

Use this only after one session format is working.

- **Promise:** a small, clear learning or reflection experience; never a guaranteed personal outcome.
- **Container:** attendee cap, length, online/in-person format, accessible materials, and a closing plan.
- **Flow:** welcome and boundary → one focused practice → voluntary reflection → close and next step.
- **Aftercare:** send a simple recap and resources; do not expose one participant’s story to another.

Pilot with a small group, collect consented feedback, and adjust one variable at a time.
