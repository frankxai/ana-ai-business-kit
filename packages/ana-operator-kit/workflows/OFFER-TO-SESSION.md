# Offer → session workflow

1. Name the offer in plain language.
2. State one client-facing outcome and one explicit boundary.
3. Choose a length, format, and what the client should bring.
4. Draft an agenda with no more than three phases: arrive, explore, leave with one next step.
5. Prepare three open questions; do not pre-decide the client’s answer.
6. Create a simple welcome and request form from the Client Session Kit.
7. Review everything for tone, privacy, and unsupported claims before sending.

Example boundary: “This is a reflective conversation and planning space. It is not therapy, diagnosis, medical, legal, or employment advice.”
