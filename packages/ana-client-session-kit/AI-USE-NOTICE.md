# Optional AI-use notice

The practitioner may use AI as a private drafting and organization aid for materials they review themselves. AI does not make decisions, diagnose, give therapy, or communicate with clients on the practitioner’s behalf.

The practitioner should explain what material, if any, is used for drafting and offer a non-AI alternative when appropriate. Do not include sensitive information unless the practitioner has explained the process and has an appropriate basis to handle it.
